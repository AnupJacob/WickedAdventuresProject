Health and Safety has always been important in responsible adventure travel. While no activity can claim to be 100% safe, there are many ways to mitigate risk and adventure travel professionals are experts at that. The COVID-19 pandemic has added a new layer of risk both in daily life and in travel experiences, so the Adventure Travel Trade Association, along with a team of technical experts, created the Adventure Travel COVID-19 Health & Safety Guidelines using international industry standards, WHO, governments and tour operators.
